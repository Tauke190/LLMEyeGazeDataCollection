Welcome to the Speech Research Project!


You are invited to participate in a research study in human behavior and speech. This study is being conducted by researchers at New York University Abu Dhabi.
It should not take you longer than 3 minutes to complete.

To take part in the project you will need a working microphone and Chrome browser. (We will provide you with a tool to check your microphone later)

The test will be simple on your part. You will have to

  • Agree with Terms and Conditions and get your unique User Key. You will use it to log in, we will use it to check your work.
  • Once you log in, you will be redirected to the Speech Test page.
  • Read the instructions carefully as you will not be able to redo the tasks.
  • You will need around 3-4 minutes to complete all tasks. However, if you exceed 20 minutes, your results will be discarded!
  • Good Luck!
